01 / The Problem & Goal

The Hurdle of "Sales-Heavy" Fatigue

Prior to taking over, GK Hair's digital platforms were dominated by hard-sell graphics and repetitive pricing grids. This approach lacked native organic appeal, leading to a drop-off in organic reach, low brand alignment with premium salon partners, and high banner fatigue among target audiences.

"We had to transition from telling consumers to buy products to showing them the luxury, high-gloss reality of GK Hair products in everyday environments."

Strategic Goals Reached

  • ✓ Convert dry, technical hair listings into organic, high-converting social-first content assets.
  • ✓ Deploy hyper-realistic generative AI product concepts with flawless shadows, lighting, and brand colors.
  • ✓ Formulate structured multi-channel campaign automation frameworks (Halloween, Early Black Friday, etc.).
Main KPI Focus Click-Through Rate & Brand Sentiment
